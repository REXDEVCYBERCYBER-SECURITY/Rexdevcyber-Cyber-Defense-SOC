# Security Policy

## REXDEVCYBER Security Policy

At REXDEVCYBER, we take the security of our next-generation cybersecurity platform and our users' data extremely seriously. This document outlines our security practices, vulnerability disclosure process, and commitments to maintaining a high-tech, secure environment.

## Supported Versions

We provide security updates for the following versions:

| Version | Supported          |

| < 1.0.0 | :x:                |

## Our Security Commitments

- **Advanced HUD & Interface Integrity**: Our holographic interfaces and glassmorphism panels are designed with security-first UI/UX to prevent clickjacking and visual spoofing.
- **AI Cyber Defense**: We utilize the REXDEVCYBER AI Engine to monitor threats in real-time, as seen in our SOC dashboards.
- **End-to-End Encryption**: All sensitive telemetry and audit logs are encrypted using modern cryptographic standards (AES-256-GCM).
- **Glassmorphism Panels**: Secure data isolation in all HUD elements.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability within REXDEVCYBER, please do not disclose it publicly. We follow a strict coordinated vulnerability disclosure (CVD) process.

1. **Detection**: Identify the potential threat or bug.
2. **Documentation**: Capture logs, screenshots, or POC (Proof of Concept) payloads.
3. **Submission**: Email our SOC team at `security@rexdevcyber.example` (Placeholder).
4. **Analysis**: Our AI defense system will triage the report within 24 hours.
5. **Resolution**: We aim to deploy patches within 72 hours for critical threats.
